Controls

Click on the settings gear to select different colors and some debugging parameters.

Click on the example name to pick a different vector field.

The following buttons are

  • clear all motes from the flow.
  • choose the color of the next mote to be added to the flow.
  • choose whether clicking on the main window will add a single mote, a stream of motes, or a grid of motes.
  • start or pause the flow.
  • choose the speed fo the flow.

There are two checkboxes to control whether the isoclines are drawn, and whether the vector field is drawn.

In the center of the window is the current flow.

If the vector field has a parameter, then there will be a slide bar at the bottom of the window to control the value of the parameter.
